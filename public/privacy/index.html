MnQ Golf

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Overview

MnQ Golf ("we," "us," or "the app") is a private recreational golf league management app for tracking schedules, scores, standings, and related league activity. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using MnQ Golf, you agree to the practices described here.

MnQ Golf is intended for use by adults participating in or following the league. The app does not sell your personal information, does not display advertising, and does not use any analytics beyond what is provided by our backend service provider (Google Firebase).

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate the league app:

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google or with an email address and password, we receive your email address and, if you use Google Sign-In, basic Google profile information such as your name and profile photo. This is used to create and identify your account.
  • League data. Information related to league play, including your player name, golf scores, match results, attendance, and standings. This is the core content of the app and is visible to other members of the league.
  • Push notification tokens. If you enable notifications, we store a device push token (provided by Firebase Cloud Messaging) so we can send you league-related notifications. This token is tied to your device and is removed when you disable notifications or it becomes invalid.
  • Technical information. Our backend provider (Firebase) may process standard technical data such as device and connection information as part of delivering the service securely.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and authenticate your sign-in.
  • To operate league features: schedules, scoring, standings, and stats.
  • To send push notifications you have opted in to receive (for example, when weekly results are finalized or a scorecard is ready to attest).
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the app.

How Your Information Is Stored and Shared

Your information is stored using Google Firebase (Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Firebase Cloud Messaging), which acts as our service provider and processes data on our behalf. Google's handling of this data is governed by its own privacy and security terms.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising or marketing. Your league data (such as your player name, scores, and standings) is visible to other members of your league as part of the app's normal function. We may disclose information if required by law.

Push Notifications

Push notifications are optional. You can enable or disable them at any time from within the app (More → Notifications) or in your device settings. Disabling notifications removes the associated device token from our records.

Data Retention

We retain your account and league information for as long as your account remains active or as needed to operate the league. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal information by contacting us at the email below.

Your Choices and Rights

  • You can access and update certain account information within the app.
  • You can disable push notifications at any time.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

Security

We rely on Google Firebase's security infrastructure to protect your information in transit and at rest. While no system can be guaranteed completely secure, we take reasonable measures to safeguard your data.

Children's Privacy

MnQ Golf is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
